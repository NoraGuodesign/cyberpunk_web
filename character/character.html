CHARACTERS

Meet the diverse cast of characters that populate the neon-lit streets of Night City. From mercenaries to corpo executives, each has their own agenda in this dystopian future.

CHARACTERS OVERVIEW

From fixers to netrunners, each soul is both predator and prey in a city that devours dreams. Gritty, chaotic, and relentlessly human, their stories intertwine in a symphony of violence and fleeting hope beneath the city's electric sky.
