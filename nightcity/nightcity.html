NIGHT CITY

Explore the sprawling metropolis of Night City, a megalopolis at the heart of the Free State of Northern California.

CITY OVERVIEW

Night City is a dystopian urban jungle that thrives on violence, oppression, and cybernetic body modification. Founded by Richard Night in 1994, it has evolved into a corporate-controlled urban sprawl where the divide between rich and poor has never been wider.

CITY CENTER
The heart of Night City, filled with towering corporate skyscrapers, luxurious hotels, and exclusive clubs for the wealthy elite.
WATSON
Once a prosperous district, now a maze of cluttered streets dominated by Asian corporations and home to numerous immigrant communities.
HEYWOOD
A populous district with a strong Latino presence and the headquarters for various gangs including the Valentinos.
WESTBROOK
The most affluent district of Night City, home to the wealthy elite and Japanese corporations.
PACIFICA
An abandoned entertainment district that was never finished, now controlled by gangs and considered the most dangerous part of Night City.
SANTO DOMINGO
The industrial heart of Night City, filled with factories, power plants, and working class housing projects.
NAVIGATE NIGHT CITY
Night City is divided into six distinct districts, each with its own unique character, architecture, and threats. From the corporate towers of City Center to the dangerous abandoned construction sites of Pacifica, each area offers new adventures and challenges.
The city is constantly evolving, with gangs fighting for territory, corporations vying for power, and everyday citizens just trying to survive in a world where cybernetic enhancements often blur the line between human and machine.
